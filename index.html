How does this work?

Send a referral
Tap the Email Referral button and an email will be generated with information about Lake Country Insurance Services. Whoever is entered into the To: will receive the information, and Lake Country Insurance Services will have both your email address and your referral's email address for tracking purposes.

Send this app
Tap the Send this app button to generate an email with a link for downloading this very app!

Contact
The buttons under the Contact heading allow for contact with Lake Country Insurance Services, including email, our website and our facebook page.

Make a Claim
The Make a Claim page has all the phone numbers you need to make a claim through your insurance provider. Just find your carrier and tap their number to call.

Agents
The Agents page gives you direct access to contacting any Lake Country Insurance Serives Agents.

App Examples
The buttons under App Examples include examples of several of our apps in action. These are weblinks to the live apps online as well as the instructional landing page for each. They provide an excellent reference for a potential customer to see our applications in action.

Get Started on Your App!
The buttons under Get Started on Your App! includes an expandable form for a potential client to enter all of their information to get their own custom application. Follow the instructions in the form and capture all of the necessary data for best results.

Get Your Own App
This button sends and email request for more information about getting your own app. It's semi-redundant, but is included in all of our apps.
