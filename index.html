How does this work?

Send a referral
Tap the "Email Referral" button and enter the email address of the person you want to refer into the "Referral Email" field, enter your name into the "Your Name" field and if you would like to include any other information enter it into the "Message" field. Once you are done tap the "Submit" button and an email will be sent to the email address provided. An email alert will also be sent to Lake Country for tracking purposes.

Send this app
Tap the "Send this app" button and enter the email address of the person you want to send the app into the "Email" field and enter your name into the "Your Name" field. Once you are done tap the "Submit" button and an email will a link to the apps Landing Page be sent to the email address provided. An email alert will also be sent to Lake Country for tracking purposes.

Contact
The buttons under the Contact heading allow for contact with Lake Country Insurance Services, including email, our website and our facebook page.

Make a Claim
The Make a Claim page has all the phone numbers you need to make a claim through your insurance provider. Just find your carrier and tap their number to call.

Agents
The Agents page gives you direct access to contacting any Lake Country Insurance Serives Agents.

Get Your Own App
Tap the "Get your own app!" button and enter the email address of the person interested in getting more information about apps from App-Platform.net in the "Email" field. Once you are done tap the "Submit" button and a representative of App-Platform.net will be in touch.

Learn more
Go to App-Platform.net to learn more about this app and others.
